We are pleased to announce the **5th OntoPortal Alliance Workshop**, to be held in **Paris, France, from September 21-25, 2026**, hosted by the [Observatoire de Paris](https://www.obspm.fr) (OntoPortal-Astro team).

As with previous editions, the workshop will gather Alliance members and invited guests for several days of technical, content, and management sessions. More details on the program and public sessions will be announced in the coming months.

We are pleased to invite you to the **public session of the OntoPortal Workshop 2026**, which will take place online on **Wednesday, September 23, 2026, from 14:00 to 17:00 CEST**.

## Draft programme

- Introduction to OntoPortal and open Q&A (40 min)
- Panel discussion
  - How to use OntoPortal?
  - OntoPortal and research infrastructures
  - OntoPortal and data spaces
  - OntoPortal and AI
  - OntoPortal and you
- Hands-on tutorial

Whether you are already using an OntoPortal appliance or are simply interested in semantic technologies for FAIR data and knowledge graphs, this session will provide an opportunity to discover the ecosystem, hear from community members, and discuss future directions.

**Registration is free but required.** https://zfrmz.eu/bAXGoKYQgWZwTw27fEPr
After registration, participants will automatically receive the Zoom connection details by email.

We hope you will be able to join us and look forward to welcoming you online on September 23.
